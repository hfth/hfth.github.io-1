For over 20 years, Hammond and Associates has provided political strategy and fundraising initiatives for a myriad of congressional, senatorial and gubernatorial campaigns across the country. Whether it's an incumbent, open-seat or challenger candidate, we provide the experience, attention, and results necessary to win in competitive races. Our methods include PAC fundraising, in-district fundraising consulting, database management, and social strategic media outreach, Additionally, we take advantage of major donor clubs in the Washington metropolitan area and across the country to ensure our clientele win in increasingly competitive races.

KJN Occasions is an all-inclusive event planning arm of Hammond and Associates that will alleviate your firm’s time consuming need to plan and execute your PAC fundraising, party planning, and diverse meeting needs. No event is too small or too big for KJN Occasions. We can meet all your needs whether they are a one-time gathering of clients for a special occasion, on-going events throughout the year or even a fundraising mailing that needs to get out the door. KJN Occasions will get the job done from start to finish, including designing and distributing invitations; dealing with caterers and outside venues, writing and distributing PAC member fundraising letters, and working alongside your client’s PAC Fundraiser for in-house events.
